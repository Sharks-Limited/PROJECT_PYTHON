Rules and Guidelines:

  • Participants must follow the instructions of the trainers at all times.
  • Appropriate workout attire is required.
  • Do not use equipment without proper knowledge or supervision.
  • Clean and return equipment to its designated place after use.
  • Refrain from loud or disruptive behavior in the gym area.
  • Personal belongings should be stored in the designated lockers.
  • Food and beverages are not allowed in the gym.
  • Use a towel to wipe down equipment after use.
  • Respect the scheduled training times and reservations.
  • Report any equipment malfunctions or issues to the staff.

By registering, you agree to adhere to the above rules and guidelines. Failure to comply may result in suspension or termination of gym privileges.
